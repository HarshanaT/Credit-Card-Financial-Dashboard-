# Credit Card Transaction Report Dashboard

## Overview

The **Credit Card Transaction Report Dashboard** provides a
comprehensive analysis of credit card performance, focusing on revenue
generation, transaction volumes, interest earnings, and customer
spending patterns.

The dashboard helps business stakeholders understand customer behaviour,
identify high-performing segments, and make data-driven decisions
related to credit card strategy.

------------------------------------------------------------------------

## Key Performance Indicators (KPIs)

The dashboard highlights the following major metrics:

-   **Total Revenue:** 55.32M
    -   Represents the overall revenue generated through credit card
        transactions.
-   **Transaction Amount:** 45M
    -   Indicates the total value of credit card transactions processed.
-   **Total Interest Earned:** 7.84M
    -   Shows the income generated through interest charges.
-   **Transaction Count:** 656K
    -   Represents the total number of credit card transactions.

------------------------------------------------------------------------

## Dashboard Features

### 1. Quarterly Revenue and Transaction Analysis

This section compares revenue performance and transaction volumes across
different quarters.

**Insights provided:** - Quarterly revenue trends - Changes in
transaction activity - Identification of high-performing periods

------------------------------------------------------------------------

### 2. Revenue by Card Category

The dashboard analyses revenue contribution by card type:

-   Blue Card
-   Silver Card
-   Gold Card
-   Platinum Card

This helps identify which card categories generate the highest revenue
and interest income.

------------------------------------------------------------------------

### 3. Revenue by Expenditure Type

Customer spending patterns are analysed across categories such as:

-   Bills
-   Entertainment
-   Fuel
-   Grocery
-   Food
-   Travel

This provides insights into customer purchasing behaviour and the most
valuable spending segments.

------------------------------------------------------------------------

### 4. Customer Demographic Analysis

The dashboard evaluates revenue contribution based on customer
characteristics:

#### Education Level

Segments include: - Graduate - High School - Unknown - Uneducated -
Post-Graduate - Doctorate

#### Employment Type

Segments include: - Businessman - White-collar employees -
Self-employed - Government employees - Blue-collar employees - Retirees

These insights support customer segmentation and targeted marketing
strategies.

------------------------------------------------------------------------

### 5. Transaction Channel Analysis

Transactions are analysed based on payment method:

-   Swipe transactions
-   Chip transactions
-   Online transactions

This helps understand customer preference for different payment
channels.

------------------------------------------------------------------------

### 6. Gender-Based Analysis

The dashboard includes revenue comparison between:

-   Female customers
-   Male customers

This supports customer profiling and targeted engagement strategies.

------------------------------------------------------------------------

## Filters Available

Users can interact with the dashboard using:

-   **Date Range Filter:** Allows selection of specific transaction
    periods.
-   **Quarter Filter:** Enables quarterly performance comparison.
-   **Gender Filter:** Allows analysis based on customer gender.

------------------------------------------------------------------------

## Business Value

This dashboard enables organizations to:

-   Monitor credit card revenue performance.
-   Identify profitable customer segments.
-   Understand spending trends.
-   Improve marketing campaigns.
-   Optimize credit card product strategies.
-   Support data-driven financial decisions.
